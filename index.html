AgriCulture

Agriculture is one of the many things in the world taking part regenerating in terms of how most people practicing agriculture nowadays use technology. Despite this,technology has only enabled them in making their work easier and even though it has catered for some of the chalenges they face,there is still more to be done on that sector.Climate change is one of the chalenges affecting farmers and even though there are some solutions that have been solved using technology such as compesating for the farmers affected by the factors caused by climate change and also creating online apps that farmers can sell their products to companies such as Twiga,there are more problems than just lack of market and climate changes.

There are Many problems being faced in Agriculture and some of them include:

  • High cost of Production
  • Delay in supply chain
  • Climatical changes that affect the normal yielding of products
  • Getting low quality products at a high price
  • Need to meet rising demand for more food of higher quality

However there are some of the challenges Kenya face to make sure Kenyan farmers have the very best.Some of the challenges are however irreversable.For example statistics show that 1.4 millio tones of soil has been washed away by water in west pokot and intensified by draught and climate.These are things that have already occured and cannot be reversed but something can be done to improve the current status.

We have an Agricultural app that help farmers have their products bought at affordable prices.This is one of our main focus since we believe that most of the farmers are drifting from agriculture because of the amount they get after selling their products despite putting in the work.We want to maintain our farmers and help in cattering for the high demand high quality products which we believe we can give.
